About us



Steam

Steam is an online distribution platform, digital rights management, multiplayer video game, and internet-based social communication service developed by Valve Corporation. Steam provides users with the ability to install and automatically update games on multiple computers, and community features such as friend and group lists, cloud storage, and game functionality. chat and converse while playing games. The software makes available a free application programming interface (API) called Steamworks, with which developers can use it to integrate many of Steam's functions into their products, including communication social and matchmaking, in-game achievements, purchases, and support for user-generated content through the Steam Workshop.

With nearly 30,000 games from AAA to indie and everything in-between. Enjoy exclusive deals, automatic game updates, and other great perks. Steam is the ultimate destination for playing, discussing, and creating games.
